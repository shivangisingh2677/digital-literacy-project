 Prevention Checklist

## 🔒 General Safety Tips
- Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone
- Always verify unknown calls or messages before responding
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown apps
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Regularly update your apps and devices for security

## 💳 UPI & Financial Safety
- Never approve a payment request unless you are sure you are paying
- Remember: Receiving money does NOT require entering a UPI PIN
- Double-check QR codes before scanning
- Use only official and trusted payment apps

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7)
